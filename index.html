About

Join us to collectively travel along the Mississippi River and its tributaries, dive deep in to the stories and content produced from all the people who are related to the land and water.

Use the rivers to navigate yourself through the basin, comment on the river, set markers or read the stories surrounding it.

Project by Kerstin Humm, Marielouis Hippler and Tilmann Finner
If you got a story to tell, want to give feedback or have a bug to report: Contact us via E-Mail.
